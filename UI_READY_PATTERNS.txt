REGEX PATTERNS - READY TO COPY-PASTE INTO UI
==============================================

Just select the pattern, copy (Cmd+C), and paste (Cmd+V) into the UI.
These have SINGLE backslashes - ready to use!

==============================================

1. HDFC BANK - DEBIT
SMS: Your A/c XX1234 debited for Rs.5,000.00 on 15-Jan-24 at POS 423156XXXXXX9876 at AMAZON RETAIL. Avl Bal: Rs.45,230.50. Not you? Call 18002586161

PATTERN:
Your A/c (?<account>\w+) debited for Rs\.(?<amount>[\d,]+\.?\d*) on (?<date>[\d-A-Za-z]+).*?at (?<merchant>[A-Z\s]+)\. Avl Bal: Rs\.(?<balance>[\d,]+\.?\d*)
